# Typography Notes

## Role of Typography

Typography is optional in this style.
When present, it should feel decorative, mystical, psychedelic, or fantasy-adjacent.

## Typographic Modes

### 1. Psychedelic Curvilinear

Use for:
- more 70s vibe
- flowing mystical titles
- poster tribute pieces

### 2. Fantasy Serif
Use for:
- sorceress
- dragon
- unicorn
- moonlit gothic poster themes

### 3. Chunky Glow Display
Use for:
- novelty-shop poster energy
- louder 80s-adjacent titles

## Typography Rules

- never let text overpower the central image unless explicitly making a poster layout
- use glow carefully
- keep text readable from distance
- avoid modern sans-serif app-branding feel
- avoid hyper-clean minimal typography

## Surface Effects for Text

Allowed:
- glow edge
- soft airbrush fade
- light gradient fill
- faint star or shimmer integration

Avoid:
- flat UI gradients
- glassmorphism
- modern tech branding styles
