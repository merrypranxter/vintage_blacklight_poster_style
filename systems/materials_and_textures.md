# Materials and Textures

## Primary Surface Illusions

### Velvet Void
The dark field should feel plush, absorbent, and infinite.

Traits:
- deep black
- light-absorbing
- soft edge interactions

### Airbrush Glow
Smooth transitions with no hard digital banding.

Traits:
- feathered gradients
- soft chroma diffusion
- bloom-like softness

### Fluorescent Pigment
Color should feel deposited like reactive paint.

Traits:
- high saturation
- edge intensity
- luminous but pigment-like, not LED-flat

### Sparkle Dust
Tiny bright fragments that create decorative life.

Traits:
- sharp
- sparse
- high-contrast
- used as accents

### Haze / Smoke
Atmospheric transition material.

Traits:
- semitransparent
- soft
- chromatic or near-white
- useful for framing and depth

## Secondary Texture Cues

- slight overspray feel
- analog softness
- soft noise in glow fields
- tiny gradient irregularities
- occasional soft-focus dreaminess

## Texture Warnings

Avoid:
- plastic gloss as default
- metallic chrome overuse
- crisp vector flatness
- modern social-media neon polish
