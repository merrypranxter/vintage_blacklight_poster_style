# Subject Archetypes

## 1. Cosmic Animal

Examples:
- wolf under moon
- tiger in nebula glow
- owl with aura eyes
- panther emerging from darkness

Key traits:
- high contrast
- luminous eyes
- dramatic fur edge glow
- iconic face-forward power

## 2. Celestial Feminine

Examples:
- moon maiden
- cosmic sorceress
- stargazer beauty
- dreamy goddess face

Key traits:
- flowing hair
- soft airbrush skin logic
- eye glow or halo
- decorative celestial framing

## 3. Mushroom / Nature Vision

Examples:
- oversized glowing mushrooms
- psychedelic forest portals
- aura vines and spores
- dream flora

Key traits:
- bioluminescent color
- glowing caps
- mystical atmosphere
- dark woodland void

## 4. Fantasy Beast / Spirit Guide

Examples:
- dragon
- unicorn
- spectral horse
- eagle spirit

Key traits:
- symbolic posture
- strong silhouette
- magical energy effects
- poster-safe mythic drama

## 5. Cosmic Landscape Icon

Examples:
- moon over glowing mountains
- waterfall in black void
- portal sky
- floating dream terrain

Key traits:
- simplified large forms
- atmospheric glow
- limited detail zones
- decorative stars and haze

## 6. Occult Symbol Assembly

Examples:
- zodiac wheel
- radiant eye
- crystal altar
- magical sigil field

Key traits:
- icon clarity
- concentric glow
- symmetry control
- restrained ornament
