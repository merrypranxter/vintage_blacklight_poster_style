# Style Pillars

## Pillar 1: Darkness as Amplifier

Black is not background filler.
It is the force that makes the colors scream.

Requirements:
- large dark areas
- restrained clutter in the outer field
- glow should emerge from darkness, not cover all darkness

## Pillar 2: Fluorescence Over Naturalism

Colors should behave like reactive pigments, not daylight surfaces.

Requirements:
- non-natural shadows
- aggressively saturated transitions
- theatrical hot/cool pairings
- artificial radiance

## Pillar 3: Central Poster Readability

The image must read from across the room.

Requirements:
- one dominant subject or dominant symbolic cluster
- strong silhouette
- high-value focal anchors
- controlled detail density

## Pillar 4: Aura and Emission

The image should look energized.

Requirements:
- halo edges
- bloom fields
- soft glow stacking
- spectral contour logic
- radiating or emanating visual behavior

## Pillar 5: Mystical / Fantasy Charge

The subject matter should feel spiritually, erotically, cosmically, or mythically loaded.

Requirements:
- iconic symbolism
- celestial or dream content
- emotionally heightened presentation
- not casual realism

## Pillar 6: Airbrushed Softness

Even when sharp accents exist, the general rendering should retain softness in transitions.

Requirements:
- gradient blending
- haze layers
- smoke / mist
- feathered luminance edges

## Pillar 7: Decorative Theatricality

This style likes excess, but not random excess.

Requirements:
- sparkles
- stars
- aura filigree
- glow rings
- ornamental framing motifs
- visual drama
