# Poster Recipe 02 — Celestial Maiden

## Goal

Create a moon-maiden blacklight poster with strong mystical glamour.

## Subject
A cosmic feminine face with flowing luminous hair.

## Composition
- centered face
- orbiting crescent moons
- stars and aura haze framing the head

## Palette
- hot magenta
- violet
- cyan
- white accents
- deep black background

## Glow Logic
- soft skin radiance
- cyan-white eye accents
- halo around hair
- subtle moon glows

## Texture
- airbrushed gradients
- soft overspray
- faint atmospheric mist

## Prompt Example
Vintage 1970s–1980s blacklight poster, a celestial moon maiden with flowing ultraviolet hair
floating in deep black velvet darkness, hot magenta, violet, cyan, and white glow palette,
crescent moons orbiting around her, soft stars and aura haze, painterly airbrush transitions,
mystical fantasy glamour, iconic wall-poster composition, fluorescent dream radiance
