# Poster Recipe 01 — Cosmic Wolf

## Goal
Create a classic blacklight wolf poster with strong moonlit fluorescence.

## Subject
A forward-facing wolf head with luminous eyes.

## Composition
- central icon
- large face
- full moon behind head
- sparse stars around outer field

## Palette
- electric blue
- violet
- magenta accents
- cyan eye highlights
- black background

## Glow Logic
- bright cyan eyes
- blue-violet rim glow on fur edges
- soft moon halo
- sparse white star points

## Texture
- airbrushed fur softness
- slight haze around moon
- black velvet field

## Prompt Example
Vintage 1970s–1980s blacklight poster, a large glowing wolf head centered against a black
velvet void, full moon halo behind the head, electric blue and violet fluorescent fur-edge lighting,
cyan luminous eyes, soft magenta accents, airbrushed mystical glow, sparse white stars,
dreamy fantasy wall-poster composition, iconic silhouette, ultraviolet bloom, painterly blacklight
atmosphere
