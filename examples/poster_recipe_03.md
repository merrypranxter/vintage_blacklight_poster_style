# Poster Recipe 03 — Mushroom Portal

## Goal
Create a blacklight mushroom poster that feels mystical, fluorescent, and dreamlike.

## Subject
A cluster of oversized glowing mushrooms rising from a dark forest void.

## Composition
- central mushroom cluster
- small portal glow behind them
- drifting spores
- subtle silhouette foliage

## Palette
- hot pink
- orange
- yellow
- acid green accents
- electric blue shadows
- black field

## Glow Logic
- bright cap edges
- warm bloom around stems
- cool blue shadow contrast
- scattered luminous spores

## Texture
- soft haze
- painterly bloom
- black velvet darkness

## Prompt Example
Vintage 1970s–1980s blacklight poster, oversized glowing mushrooms rising from a black velvet
forest void, hot pink, orange, yellow, acid green, and electric blue fluorescent palette, soft aura
fog, luminous drifting spores, mystical portal glow behind the cluster, airbrushed psychedelic
fantasy atmosphere, iconic wall-poster readability, dreamy fluorescent bloom
