# Era Cues

## 1970s Cues

Typical signals:
- heavier psychedelic carryover
- denser ornamental curves
- softer painterly fantasy
- mushrooms, zodiac, astrology, cosmic femininity
- big cats, wolves, eagles, moon scenes
- warmer analog-feeling gradients
- hand-painted vibe

## 1980s Cues

Typical signals:
- cleaner airbrush transitions
- more hard-edged glow accents
- more fantasy-paperback overlap
- more chrome-adjacent highlights
- stronger electric blue, magenta, cyan combinations
- more “neon energy” without fully becoming synthwave

## Shared Cross-Era Traits

- blacklight-reactive logic
- wall-art readability
- dreamlike staging
- fantasy/occult/cosmic motifs
- soft bloom and glow
- iconic focal hierarchy

## Era Blend Guidance

For a balanced 70s–80s hybrid:
- keep 70s mysticism and symbolic softness
- add 80s electric edge clarity
- preserve painterly glow rather than digital sleekness
